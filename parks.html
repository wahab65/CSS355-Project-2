Bryant Park

  • site of ice skating at Winter Village
  • free summer movies on the lawn
  • endless meals and meetings at its tables
  • an ideal resting spot for the thousands of tourists and residents
  • outdoor Reading Room has picture books and summertime story times
  • purchase food and drink at the park OR bring your own
  • enjoy the skaters and festive views
  • no reservations needed

Hudson River Park Trust

  • waterfront park on the North River
  • numerous different piers/places to explore for different activies
  • panoramic views of Lower Manhattan and the New York Harbor from Pier 25
  • meandering boardwalk through native plants, a street-style skatepark, dog run, basketball and tennis courts along lively stretch of waterfront at Tribeca Upland
  • catch some rays while lounging on a large, green lawn, at Pier 45
  • Pier 51 playground offers imaginative play with a nautical theme for children
