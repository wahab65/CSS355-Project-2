Who We Are?



Pick Your Place is a startup based in New York. We suggest unique and exciting destinations from different categories based on real reviews. With Pick Your Place, you can spend less time searching and more time enjoying the experiences that matter to you.

What We Do?



We always have a hard time finding places to go to because they don't turn out as expected or sometimes we just want to try/explore something different. We know you also struggle with this, so, Pick Your Place suggests you several places from different categories and gives real reviews from others like you. We do the research so you can spend your time enjoying!
